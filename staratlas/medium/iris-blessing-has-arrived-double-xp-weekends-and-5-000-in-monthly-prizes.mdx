Iris’ Blessing calls out to citizens all over Galia. Riches, luck and fun are in everyone’s future and you’re not going to want to miss it! For the next six months, enjoy double XP weekends and monthly drawings worth over $30,000!

## Double XP Weekends — The More You Play, the More You Earn!

Due to popular demand, we’re bringing back double XP weekends! Mark your calendars for the below 6 weekends where you’ll be able to earn DOUBLE the Ink. Play in official gunplay and racing matches, and for every 100 XP, earn 1 Ink!

## Double XP Weekends

- **Double XP Weekend #1:** October 24, 2025 — October 26, 2025
- **Double XP Weekend #2:** November 28, 2025 — November 30, 2025
- **Double XP Weekend #3:** December 26, 2025 — December 28, 2025
- **Double XP Weekend #4:** January 23, 2026 — January 25, 2026
- **Double XP Weekend #5:** February 27, 2026 — March 1, 2026
- **Double XP Weekend #6:** March 27, 2026 — March 29, 2026

Earning double XP in Star Atlas will soon tie directly into the z.ink airdrop, meaning during these weekends, you’ll earn double the XP, double the Ink, and double the z.ink!

## $30,000 in Rewards — Monthly Discord Drawings!

Every second Friday of the month, you’ll have a chance to turn in tickets earned from joining Discord community events! Whether it’s an Atlas Brew, an Economic Forum, or a Game Night, collect your ticket and submit it through our Discord bot for a chance to win your share of the monthly drawing! Keep an eye out for exclusive challenges and activities that will give out tickets as well!

## How to Earn Tickets and Submit Them into the Monthly Drawing

1. Join community events like the Atlas Brew or Star Atlas Economic Forum
1. Attendees will be given a code to submit into the Discord bot (Sola Qint)
1. Type /claim or head to the #sola-qint-bot channel to submit your code
1. The Discord bot (Sola Qint) will give you tickets
1. Your tickets will auto submit into the monthly drawing
