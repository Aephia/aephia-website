*The award-winning StarAtlas.com website experience brings the blockchain gaming metaverse on Solana a victory in the Awwwards Users’ Choice category.*

![StarAtlas.com Website /// Award-winning Digital Experience /// Metaverse Information Hub](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/f3406f41197de3974759ad5ba9be7f4a1869e8e9ccab8633a7c90111d54f9769.jpeg)

The new [site](https://staratlas.com/), which launched on August 25, 2021 along with a cinematic [teaser trailer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hnFNh-1qLWk), has achieved worldwide acclaim by being proclaimed an [Awwwards](https://www.awwwards.com/annual-awards-2021/site-of-the-year-users-choice) Site Of The Year.

The StarAtlas.com website began its victorious journey by first achieving the title of the *Website of the Day*, then proceeded by being selected as the *Website of the Month*, and was subsequently declared a[ nominee](https://annual.awwwards.com/siteoftheyear-nominees/star-atlas) for the annual award among many other great works.

The stars aligned for Star Atlas and [Hello Monday](https://www.hellomonday.com/), a US-Danish digital studio, in creating an award-winning immersive website experience that conveys the depth and quality players can look forward to in the triple-A title.

> On behalf of the team creating our blockchain gaming metaverse, I’d like to thank our community for sharing the vision of an immersive open world the size of a galaxy and building the future with us. I also want to thank the creative team at Hello Monday for helping us manifest and condense this intergalactic vision in the form of a website on StarAtlas.com. It’s a great honor to receive this award and be recognized as a driving force in inspiring people around the world.

> -Pablo Quiroga, CRO and Co-founder of Star Atlas

The StarAtlas.com website features the immersive experience, a [showroom](https://staratlas.com/showroom), and a repository of fresh information about the making of a metaverse as well the team behind it.

> The seamless immersive experience Hello Monday has put together to allow us to dive into the world of Star Atlas is incredible. Using simple mechanics, the website adds a consistent depth to the game, enlarging its universe and giving it a voice. While also displaying layouts and interactive visuals that are real eye candy.

> — [Bastien Allard](https://www.awwwards.com/margherita-fortuna/), Awwwards Jury member

## ABOUT STAR ATLAS

Star Atlas is a next-gen gaming metaverse emerging from the confluence of state of the art blockchain, real-time graphics, multiplayer video game, and decentralized financial technologies.

Real-time graphics technology using Unreal Engine 5’s Nanite allows for cinematic quality video game visuals. Blockchain technology using the[ Solana](http://solana.com) protocol established a largely serverless and secured gameplay experience. Non-fungible tokens obtained and traded within Star Atlas creates an economy that replicates the tangibility of real world assets and ownership.
