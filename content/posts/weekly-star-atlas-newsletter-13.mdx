## Assets

A total of 51 ships will be released this year. The first ship will be released on February 3 and this will be the Fimbul XXS and will cost $15. The team is expecting to release a new ship every one to two weeks. 

There will be three titan ships that will be released via a reverse Dutch Auction mechanism:
- Pearce T1 - Titan Fighter
- Fimbul ECOS Superphoenix - Titan Bomber
- Bussan The Last Stand mk VIII - Titan Fighter

Unlike the initial wave of GAO ships that was accompanied by a brochure, this next wave of ships does not yet have a brochure tied to it.

## CREAM v0

For this module the team is working with multiple squads on:
- mining & extraction
- crafting & recipes
- space exploration feature (allowing  ships to be used in a different way than just putting them into SCORE)

CREAM is expected to launch in July.

## Development

The team wanted to remind everyone that a lot of the visuals they shared and will be sharing are subject to change. Additionally, they shared the phases that they are practicing:
- discovery: talk about what the project is, what it should be doing
- exploration: wireframes, prototyping
- validation: use prototypes and community feedback to help the team understand how we use those features
- Production

Because of this, target dates have been removed from the roadmap. Going forward, they will inform us on the phase the project is in. Before releasing any dates, they will follow the process of the dev-workflow and then share the confirmed date.
