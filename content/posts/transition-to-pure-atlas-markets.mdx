In the beginning of the Galactic Asset Offerings, ships in Star Atlas were sold in USDC. Later in the year, the team started to offer half of the supply of a new ship in ATLAS. CEO Michael Wagner now revealed that this development could continue in the current year:

“We will be introducing a transition to pure ATLAS markets for new asset drops over the course of this year. The first modification to the ATLAS vs. USDC markets will be introduced with our first ship drop this year.”
