Participants of the ReBirth event received a variety of badges as rewards, that promise to “activate access to future perks and events. Rewarded to those who participated in the ReBirth event.” Asked about the function of those badges, Star Atlas CEO Michael Wagner told the community that those badges will be associated with “some minor boosts. Benefits will scale to exclusivity.”
