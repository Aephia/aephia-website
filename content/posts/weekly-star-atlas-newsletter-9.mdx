As mentioned in a previous issue, Star Atlas partnered with Sperasoft to work together in developing the Unreal Engine 5 version of the game. Just recently, a [Medium article](https://medium.com/star-atlas/star-atlas-partners-with-sperasoft-immersive-metaverse-exploration-game-2037ae0d6666) was released to provide more depth on this ground-breaking partnership. For more information about Sperasoft, visit [https://sperasoft.com/](https://sperasoft.com/).
