## z.ink A.LPHA Airdrop Campaign

Last weekâ€™s [Economic Forum](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EeowL8NWaag) was mostly focused on the [z.ink test campaign](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/z-ink-test-airdrop-season-the-guide/), and Chris (VP Game Economy) dropped a few interesting tidbits.

First off, he shared the following numbers (this past Thursday):

* There are 1588 zAccounts, of which
* 844 had earned XP (so are active), and
* 726 had linked their Epic Games account (UE5)

He also noted that activity has picked up across the board since the A.LPHA campaign launched, including GM purchases, and in-game activity in both SAGE and Holosim.

Chris shared that about **3.2 million zXP** is required to hit level 20\. Note that he did pluck this number from the top of his head, so he was not 100% sure. Knowing this number allows everyone to perform simple math to figure out if they are on the right track, need to pick it up, or can take it easy going forward in the A.LPHA campaign.

However, that is not so easy to do for those who are close, as a lot of weightings were changed, and corrections made, after the first week of the campaign being live:

* The team discovered that Holosim was awarding the same amount of XP as SAGE, which was obviously incorrect. SAGE should reward significantly more zXP than Holosim. This was corrected.
* They also updated the way SAGE zXP is calculated, which should increase the XP earned there slightly.
* After the first weekend, they realized that a substantial number of activities had been overlooked in the zXP reward calculations. This was fixed retroactively, which saw people accumulate a lot of zXP overnight.
* DAO locker zXP was tweaked down a little, as it accounted for 70% of all zXP rewarded (above the target of 40%).

Furthermore, Chris mentioned that the 2x zXP multiplier awarded by the APEX Subscription will likely be tuned down a little, arguing that doubling the zXP is a bit too excessive.
