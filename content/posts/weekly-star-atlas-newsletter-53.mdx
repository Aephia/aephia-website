## Fuzzy Lemur

Talking about the infrastructure part, Michael announced *Fuzzy Lemur*, a [Solana] Blockchain Indexing Service built by the team, allowing them to bypass RPC nodes and get their data directly from the source.

Early analysis shows that they are able to receive state changes from the validators in as little as 16ms, with median speeds being 215ms, making it the fastest indexer in the industry at this point in time.

Getting these interactions as fast as possible is important as the team plans to use the blockchain as their "game server(s)".

## UE5: New Roadmap

Perhaps the biggest news on Monday was the unveiling of the full, multi-year roadmap for the Unreal Engine 5 game. To bring more structure to these releases, the team identified two different phases: a *pre-season* release phase and a *seasonal* release phase. 

Below are the four releases planned for the **pre-season** phase:
1. Showroom environment
2. Flight / Race, Multiplayer Instances
3. Crafting building materials, Ship cargo & hauling
4. ECOS attack, Looting & skins

The first pre-release (R1) was launched during the last 426Live event, and the next step will be the release of the R2 release that was demoed last week.

There will be 2 more pre-season releases coming next year, after which the team will flag that phase as completed, switching to a seasonal release schedule instead.
## The Warp Gate Project

Starting with R3, the Showroom map will be greatly expanded and the players will have to work together to create the very first **megastructure** project within Star Atlas. Everyone will work collaboratively to collect materials and resources from mining facilities and contribute these to the creation of a *warp gate*. Once completed, this gate will take players out of the Showroom into the wider Star Atlas universe!

## UE5: Seasons

## Season 0

After the gate has been completed, players will enter Season 0 and the pre-release cycle will have come to an end.

Season 0 will introduce a full game loop around combat racing. It will also mark a major milestone for Star Atlas, leading them to name this release *Star Atlas: Alive*.

**Season 0 - Combat Racing**
- Racing career
- Fighter career
- SAGE (Star Atlas: Golden Era, see below)

## Season 1-8

Moving forward after the launch of Season 0, the team will release new content in a seasonal format going forward, until they've managed to complete their vision for Star Atlas v1. Every season will consist of some specialization of certain gameplay mechanics wherein they enhance one (or more) of the professions in the game.

Note that these seasons are going to take many years to roll out, with Michaels stating that they presently still expect to *need ****5 - 7 years**** to finish this roadmap*.

The seasons will bring the following to the Star Atlas UE5 experience:

**Season 1 - Ship Mining**
- Space miner career
- Crew
- ONI central space station
- Space habitats

**Season 2 - Game Hunting**
- FPS combat
- Game hunter career
- Mounting

**Season 3 - Bounty Hunting**
- Bounty hunting
- USTUR central station

**Season 4 - Smuggle & Freight**
- Smuggling career
- Freight career
- Player space station creation

**Season 5 - Land mining & Crafting**
- Mining outposts
- Manufacturing career
- MUD central space station

**Season 6 - Exploration & Transport**
- Exploration
- Passenger transport
- Real-Time Strategy: Fleet & crew command
- Homeworld planets

**Season 7 - Repair & Salvage**
- Repair career
- Salvage career
- All ships flight ready

**Season 8 - Rescue, Refuel & Politics**
- Rescue career
- Refueler career
- Politician career
- Full decentralized
- Star Atlas 1.0

Besides some new screenshots of the current state of affairs, not much was shared on SCREAM at Breakpoint. However, the team did reveal the official name for Project SCREAM on stage on Monday. Henceforth SCREAM will be called **Star Atlas: Golden Era**. The team's [Medium article](https://medium.com/star-atlas/cosmic-alpha-burst-star-atlas-unveils-roadmap-graphic-novel-sage-and-more-at-solana-breakpoint-835589ba28e7) describes it as follows:
> [...] a top-down, webGL-based, grand strategy game with some of the same game loops that will take place in later seasons of Star Atlas

On stage, Michael added that one of the reasons for calling this *Golden Era* is because it unlocks the next generation of game economics across the Star Atlas ecosystem.

## NFT Collection

If you are a collector, or you'd like to get your hands on a physical book, then you can also choose to buy a commemorative NFT for $20 (in SOL).

There are two of these NFTs, one is being sold on the [Galactic Marketplace](https://play.staratlas.com/market/4Rrcj4MArUcMtHPp6amTy3SUXbCGYG3n8sf8KgMKjRnr), and the other is sold via [Magic Eden](https://magiceden.io/launchpad/star_atlas_episode_1). Both marketplaces offer a unique collectible cover!

If you own 18 consecutive exclusive covers (from either GM or ME), you will have the option to redeem them (possibly through burning) for a physical, hard-bound copy of the complete Graphic Novel.

There are also some other benefits to buying these limited edition NFTs: You will get early access to the monthly series and on top of that, you will get expanded content.

## Faction Trivia Competition

The Community team is organizing a new competition for the community, called Faction Trivia. This event will span 3 rounds over three weeks, with the rounds taking place on November 11th, 18th, and 25th in the Star Atlas Discord voice chat.

During each of these rounds, questions will be asked, with points going to each faction that manages to answer a question correctly first.

At the end of the 3 weeks, the faction with the most points will win.
## Prizes

Each correctly answered question will yield the person answering one raffle ticket. The community members of the winning faction will see their raffle tickets double. The prizes that will be raffled out have not yet been announced.

One more special prize will go to the top scorer of the winning faction. They will get a special behind-the-scenes look at the Star Atlas [team's Discord] server.
