## DACs are Live!

Last Thursday, February 1st (February 2nd for Europeans), the team [officially launched](https://medium.com/star-atlas/the-rise-of-corporations-star-atlas-launches-dac-platform-v0-b6eb60df626a) _version 0_ of their **DAC Platform**! This has been a long-awaited and much-anticipated release!

Up until last week, several dozen guilds had already been created, but only in name. By far, most of them existed solely on Discord servers, and the community sort of agreed that this was the way to do it.

Now that the team has launched their official registration, they are opening the doors to a more seamless discovery process for Star Atlas enthusiasts and incorporating DAC affiliations in-game!

Now, this first release was a little bumpy. The team put some hotfixes in, but it may still require some refreshes before you find yourself able to join a DAC. And, with this being the V0 release, it‚Äôs also very limited at the moment. The current features are available:

* Everyone can create their own DAC (if they choose to)
* Every DAC has a profile page with a description and user-selectable list of tags
* All registered players can join DACs. You can join more than one (though your primary DAC may disapprove)
* DAC owners can approve membership requests, changing the member‚Äôs role from Applicant to Member
* There are currently only two roles available, which are auto-assigned (‚Äúapplicant‚Äù & ‚Äúmember‚Äù)

These roles will be much expanded in the future. This will, among other things, allow the DAC creator to share the burden of member approval (a pretty tedious process at present) with hand-picked members.

_Note: If you want to see what the process of joining a DAC is like, watch [Metaverse Explorer giving it a go](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ppnybLh2Trk)!_

### Rocky, but Solid Start

As alluded to earlier, the launch of the DAC Platform did not go as smoothly as one would have hoped. Despite this, a surprisingly high number of DACs have been registered meanwhile. Michael (CEO) [stated on X.com](https://x.com/at_mwagner/status/1753621545480773693?s=20) that 236 DACs registered in the first (roughly) 24 hours, alone!

Meanwhile, the team has been gathering feedback and appears to have started work on smoothening and improving the experience.

## New Ship: Ogrika Ruch

On X.com, the team announced the upcoming release of a new ship: the Ogrika Ruch! The Ruch is a single-seater XXS Racer ship from the Ogrika line (known for ships like the famous Thripid but also the slightly bigger X-Smal Niruch transporter).

The Ogrika Ruch will be available to purchase today, on **Monday, the 5th of February**!

Contrary to the Fimbul Sledbarge, released [two weeks ago](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/weekly-star-atlas-newsletter-114/), Chip (Director of Game Systems Design) has **_not_** yet updated the [ship specification spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1cfzW3uiqkbnfJexBysh0MAzZYRhiRwQg8WlsFWfuKgs/edit#gid=205337066).

Unfortunately, pricing was not yet known at the time of publication. Note that secondary market sales will not be allowed on the Galactic Marketplace for 30 days.

### Jupiter Skin

To commemorate the launch of Jupiter‚Äôs token $JUP and the big airdrop that accompanied it, the team is launching a special, limited edition skin that can only be acquired using $JUP.

For those unfamiliar with [Jupiter](https://jup.ag/), they are the leading Decentralized Exchange (DEX) aggregator on Solana. You‚Äôve likely used their services if you ever exchanged one token for another. The team has also incorporated Jupiter‚Äôs services into their own products, to enable people to easily swap to ATLAS.

### Puri Skins

As if the Jupiter skin was not already a surprise, the team revealed that they will also sell two limited-edition Puri skins! At first glance, you may think that these skins are themed around the cute(?) pet that was revealed a while back, but that is not the case here. The skins are based on the community-launched meme token of the same name!

Unlike their Jupiter counterpart, the Puri skin comes in two editions: an eye-popping pink variant and a more subdued (but still very cool-looking) black one. Like the Jupiter skin, you will need a small bag of $PURI tokens instead of ATLAS or USDC if you want to acquire one or both.

When this newsletter was published, the exact price of the skins was not yet known. So far, the team has only communicated that they did not want to share the price in advance due to the volatility of the tokens (especially PURI). These will go on sale in tandem with the Ogrika Ruch ship itself!
