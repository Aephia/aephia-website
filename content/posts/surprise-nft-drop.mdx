Yesterday, Thursday, there was an early Christmas surprise for a lot of Star Atlas players. Or – to be precise: for 6,765 of them.

They found in their wallets the “Cosmic Sounds” batch. A Collectible rated as Epic Crew Gear. _“Everyone holding a ReBirth/ Legends meta-poster or a piece from the FIRMAM3NTAL collection now has a shiny new Star Atlas NFT in their wallet!”_, wrote the Star Atlas team via Twitter.

The description of the item in the marketplace reads as follows: _“Music Festival Patch. Increases crew morale, social cohesion, and also increases your overall good fortune in the metaverse. Soundscape by BLOND:ISH.”_
