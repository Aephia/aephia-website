## Assets

Two news ships have been officially launched. These are the Ogrika Mik and the Fimbul ECOS Unibomba. Visit the Star Atlas Marketplace to get your hands on these.

An interesting thing to note on the Unibomba, is that it only carries a single (terraforming) bomb. It will need to return to a shipyard, or other ECOS ship (Bombarella or larger) to re-arm.

## Development

Michael confirmed that they have selected [Tribeca DAO](https://tribeca.so/), an already established DAO framework on the Solana chain, to aid them in the governance component. Using this existing solution allows their team to ease up on development efforts. However, custom development is still necessary since such tools do not offer all-in-one and out-of-the box solutions that satisfy their needs and requirements.
