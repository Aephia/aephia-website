## 426Live:NYK

The topic on both the team's and community's mind right now is the 426Live event that will be taking place this Thursday. ## Progress

The team did not have that much to report regarding their progress last week, so we gathered it into a single section:
## SCREAM

The past week Game Design has been working together with the Economics team on looting mechanics. The two have been refining the levers and mechanics of loot drops. Besides this, the Econimics team also defined all ATLAS sinks that are going to be present in SCREAM.

The Blockchain team has continued work on the Sector Program. Finally, the UX department has been adding fidelity to the *Outliner*, such as filtering and column sorting. Additionally they have been busy on visualizing high system density using the heads-up display.
## Other

Besides SCREAM, here is an overview of the other progress reported:
- The Showroom team has been busy finalizing their distribution preparations.
- The DAO team has been working on implementing analytics and will focus on the DAO Proposal (Flow) next.
- The Graphic Novel team has been working on the UI for comic readers.
- The Marketplace team is busy rolling out several bug fixes and improvements, including: sorting by rarity, removal of the *fixed* "1" in the input field, and removing "stuck" orders.
- The Marketplace Fees as nearly done, some work remains regarding the team's internal services that are interacting with the Solana chain.
