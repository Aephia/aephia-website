## Holosim – Season 1 Chapter 2

Chapter 2 of Holosim Season 1 has been [a long time coming](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/weekly-star-atlas-newsletter-219/). After yet another last-minute hold [last week](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/weekly-star-atlas-newsletter-219/), Michael (CEO) made it official in Discord: Chapter 2 launches **Tuesday, March 10th**! There is a countdown on the [game page](https://holosim.staratlas.com), ticking down to 18:00 UTC on that day.

Good to know: This chapter will come with prizes again, including $10k worth of assets (OMO pricing, meaning close to the actual marketprices).

This week’s [Atlas Brew](https://youtu.be/aoh4h_et39g) was almost entirely dedicated to Chapter 2, with John (Product Manager) walking through most aspects of the game while sharing some new details. 

To begin with, unlike the five weeks the team shared previously, this chapter will run for roughly **two months**. Rather than one big end-of-season payout, the team is exploring splitting the prize window into shorter 2–4 week intervals, though that hasn’t been finalised yet. Beyond the assets from the $10k prize pool, story completion will earn players a **cosmetic badge**. Note, however, that there’s no separate prize pool tied to narrative progression this time around.

The **Battle Pass** is back, and you can get it for a one-time $10 payment for the full chapter duration. It unlocks 10x daily ATLAS rewards, double crew on daily check-ins, a +35% streak bonus, and an exclusive avatar border. Z.ink subscribers get it automatically. No extra steps are needed, no matter which subscription tier you have.

The XP economy rewards bold play: you can earn up to 2 million XP from bases furthest from your Council Star System, so staying home is actively the wrong call.

Automation has been renamed **Fleet Directives**: players issue commands (mine, transport, patrol) and fleets execute them. Mining now has consequences. **NPC threat fleets** spawn based on your mining rate on a roughly 10-minute randomised timer. Destroy them, and they drop lootable cargo: R4 materials, fuel, ammo, and toolkits.

**Safe zone mining will be net negative.** It exists purely for tutorial purposes. Also, SDUs no longer drop in the safe zone from scanning. Every Starbase has **NPC-seeded markets** where all resources can be bought and sold, though at deliberately unfavourable prices. They’re the economic floor and ceiling, there to prevent total market collapse.

On the fair play side, on-chain asset transfers between accounts are tracked. Accounts found sharing resources across alts face leaderboard removal. Multi-boxing is allowed, but accounts cannot pool resources across wallets.

John also walked through what we can expect in the upcoming chapters: a combat revamp, a crew revamp, possibly crafting, and improvements to scanning. The scanning roadmap is shifting from SDUs to derelict ships and hidden asteroid fields, which may trigger NPC spawn events when discovered.

## Lore Wiki

José shared during the [Atlas Brew](https://youtu.be/aoh4h_et39g) that he plans to launch a **public Star Atlas lore wiki** very soon, possibly even this week. The wiki covers species, factions, manufacturers, geography, ages, and campaigns, much of which has never been publicly shared before. José plans to use this as the backbone for the lore he is developing for C4, and also sees possibilities for RPG campaigns, books, and novels.

## News Bits

Here are some smaller bits of news to wrap up this week’s issue:

* Michael (CEO) answered a question in the #foundation-room, noting that the internal target for the **end of the O.RIGIN campaign** is April 2026\. He immediately added that he could not provide a confidence level on that, however.
* Michael also shared in the #foundation-room that they are shifting the remaining team into a full **OpenClaw infrastructure**, saying that “Output is going to increase substantially.”
* The 0% fee promotion that launched alongside the OMO strategy in December 2025 is done. The standard **6% topline fee** is back, with ATLAS locker fee discounts reinstated.
* Chris (VP of Game Economy) had to cancel last week’s **Economic Forum** due to unexpected schedule changes. He apologised for the short notice.
* This Friday will see another round of **Iris’ Blessing raffles** take place! You have until Friday to accumulate more tickets by joining live shows/events.
