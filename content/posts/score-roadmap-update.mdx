Every Friday the Star Atlas team plans to publish a short [update](https://staratlas.notion.site/Roadmap-Report-8e75d87c5b7b4a67afb4fd776d6c3176) on the roadmap. The most important info this time: the target date (December 9, 2021) for the launch of the mini game SCORE still remains unconfirmed.

The main points of the update:

* SCORE on-chain program near completion
* SCORE UI components near completion
* Linking UI to blockchain program is in progress
* Smart contract auditing firm lined up
* No modification of any target dates, still on track with current guidance
