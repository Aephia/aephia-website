As confirmed in the latest Star Atlas townhall and also updated on the [official roadmap](https://staratlas.notion.site/staratlas/72cb710bbd894cd8819bde948e42ac31?v=5c5cca97579e4cd79089362bf03e55b1), [SCORE](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/what-is-the-star-atlas-mini-game-score/) launch date is now confirmed for the 16th of December. Players will be able to start earning income from any ships they hold. All rewards will be in [ATLAS](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/what-is-the-polis-token/) for the time being.
