More fresh news from the Star Atlas exclusive foundation room. Star Atlas CEO Michael Wagner, confirmed that tier 1 (lowest) claim stakes (land slots) will be upgradeable all the way to tier 5 (highest). In reply to a question on whether space problems, like in adjacent land slots for example, will limit the upgradeability of the land slots themselves, Michael replied that they are still discussing it, but “_most likely will not be locked out from upgrading._“
