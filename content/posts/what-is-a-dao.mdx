A [DAO, or decentralised autonomous organization](https://decrypt.co/resources/decentralized-autonomous-organization-dao), is a business structure where control is spread out rather than hierarchical.

DAOs are organized using smart contracts, with participants using governance tokens to vote on topics such as fund allocation.

A [DAC (Decentralized Autonomous Corporation](https://blog.bitnovo.com/en/what-is-a-dac/) or Decentralized Autonomous Community) is a subclass of DAOand it’s a community that works through rules encoded as smart contracts**.**
