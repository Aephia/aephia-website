## Airdrop Campaign(s)

The z.ink team (ATMTA) will airdrop between **30% and 40%** of their Total Token Supply (TTS) across four campaigns, spanning roughly 2 years. The O.rigin campaign, live now, is the first of these campaigns and will distribute the **initial 10% of the token supply** to everyone who participates and earns zXP.

_The more zXP you accumulate, the bigger your slice of this 10% pie will be!_

Please note that the number of z.ink tokens earmarked for this first airdrop is _fixed_. This means your proportional share of zXP earned relative to the total amount of zXP earned by all participants is what determines how much of the airdrop you will receive.

The campaign now knows you exist! Next, add **all other wallets** you use to **participate in the Star Atlas ecosystem**. Continue reading to learn which wallets you need to hook up. Below, we‚Äôll explain how to get those added first.

**Steps ‚Äì Adding another wallet**

1. Go to your wallet (extension) and select the wallet you want to connect

## Three zXP Tracks

The zXP you earn through various activities contributes to three different progression tracks:

1. **Zink Level** ‚Äî Your overall zProfile level. It _never resets_ and is listed on your ID Card (top-left of the dashboard).
2. **Airdrop Season Level** ‚Äî This level _resets_ with every new airdrop season. It currently tracks the O.rigin season (Season 1). This is the level shown on the leaderboard.
3. **Multipass Rank** ‚Äî Multipass operates independently of Airdrop Seasons. It comes with its own seasons, each tied to (potentially) different start and end dates, and resets every time a new season starts. Instead of levels, Multipass uses ranks. Your current rank is shown in the Multipass header on the dashboard. Note that _zXP only counts towards a Multipass rank progression when you have a live subscription_.

At this moment, this being the first airdrop season, your Zink Level and Airdrop Season Level will be the same. However, for future airdrop seasons, these will deviate. Your Zink Level will never be reset from here on out (note that during the A.lpha test campaign, there was a hard cap of level 20). Your Airdrop Season level will reset before the start of every new Airdrop Season (there are four planned at this point in time).

It‚Äôs important to realize that some tasks will take a few hours to register the activity. Let‚Äôs go over the various tasks now.

### Play Star Atlas: Golden Era

Chris, the econ wiz on the team, has carefully crafted an algorithm to reward activity in Star Atlas: Golden Era (SAGE). This means that not every interaction will reward you with zXP. In general, however, if you interact with fleets (move, mine), you should see zXP trickling in.

SAGE interaction should be picked up pretty quickly. If you interacted with the game and the task remains red for more than 2 hours, it‚Äôs time to create a ticket. That is, unless you are one of the participants with this specific setup:

**Known bug:** If your primary wallet (the wallet you used to create the zProfile) is _not_ the same wallet you use to play SAGE, your zXP will not be registered for now. The team is looking into this further in the new year.

_Note_: In the rare case that multiple Player Profiles were created on your primary wallet by SAGE (back in the day), and the wrong one was selected to detect SAGE actions, you can select the correct one by clicking your account in the top-right corner, choosing _Manage,_ and then _Change Primary Profile_.

### Play Holosim

Unfortunately, this task is currently disabled. Once Chapter 2 of the free-to-play Holosim game goes live in January 2026, this game will reward you with zXP for in-game activities, much in the same way it‚Äôs being done for SAGE.

Note that Holosim, available to play with no upfront investment, will yield much less zXP than playing a game such as SAGE.

### Lock ATLAS or POLIS tokens

If you have ATLAS or POLIS tokens, it‚Äôs best to lock them to passively earn zXP daily. **This zXP is awarded once a day, shortly after midnight UTC**.

Note that zXP is rewarded on an **asymptotic curve**, meaning the more you lock, the less zXP you earn. The team has not disclosed the exact formula, but participants observed the effect during the A.lpha campaign.

In the case of the POLIS Locker, it‚Äôs not about the amount locked, but the amount of **Polis Voting Power** (PVP) you have on any given day. This means the longer you lock your POLIS, the more zXP it will generate.

_Note_: You do not have to acquire/lock new ATLAS or POLIS to receive zXP. Anything you already locked up previously will earn you zXP. Of course, you can always lock more, but this is not necessary. It will, of course, boost your zXP earned.

## zXP Weighting & Distribution

Not all tasks are equal when it comes to earning zXP. In addition, zXP is not awarded immediately in all cases.

In the table below, we list the (rough) weighting of each task/activity and the (expected) frequency when zXP is being rewarded for the activity.

| **Activity**              | **zXP weighting** | **zXP allocation frequency** |
| ------------------------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Play Holosim              | Low               | Within minutes               |
| Play Star Atlas (UE5)     | Medium            | Every \~2 hours              |
| Stake Claim Stakes        | Medium            | 2 AM UTC (once per day)      |
| Play SAGE                 | Medium/High       | Within minutes               |
| Primary Marketplace sales | High              | Within minutes               |
| ATLAS Locked & PVP        | High              | Midnight UTC (once per day)  |

As mentioned in the previous section, tasks will turn green once the first zXP has been awarded for them. Do not panic if this does not happen immediately; the table above shows it can sometimes take a while. If a task does not turn green well after it should, please request support. See the Support section below for more information.

## Daily Check-in Bonus

Beyond subscriptions, there is another (free-of-charge) way to unlock XP multipliers. By simply hitting the Claim button in the Daily Streak area within 24-48 hours after you last did so, you can lock in another day and expand your daily streak.

![Z.ink Dashboard - Daily Streak](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/4d738118ff5c9df5f822f54389ec73500ce7f5ef79dbb7e60cf0a4690887289e.png)

Z.ink Dashboard ‚Äì Daily Streak

The first time you do this, you will right away unlock the 1% boost. After doing this 3 times, you will unlock the 5% zXP boost.

After you claim a day, you won‚Äôt be able to claim the next day within the following 24 hours. After this, you will have exactly 24 hours to make that claim. If you forget, your streak will be reset, and you will have to start anew.

### Separate Progression Track

Multipass offers a separate progression track that is independent of the airdrop track. It seeks to reward subscriptions, even when no airdrops are active. As such, its seasons will likely always overlap with airdrop seasons, but may not be fully in sync. It is unlikely that this first Multipass season will end before the O.rigin campaign ends, but it may extend beyond that date.

Multipass ranks are easier to achieve than Zink levels. This also means that smaller fish can relatively easily get these rewards!

Note that, unlike the global Zink level, your Multipass Rank will reset before a new season starts.

## FAQ

**Q: I‚Äôm renting my fleets to other players. Who is receiving the zXP from these fleets when they are used in SAGE?**
A: If you are renting your fleets out to others, those other players will earn the zXP with them, not you.

**Q: Do I need to lock new ATLAS/POLIS, or new Ships for SAGE, or anything like that? Historic achievements don‚Äôt count, after all.** 
A: There is no need whatsoever to lock new tokens or get new ships into SAGE. Historic data is disregarded means that whatever you did before the campaign started (and you created your zProfile) is ignored. But whatever you have locked now absolutely counts. And your actions in SAGE count as well, no matter if the ships are old or new.

**Q: If I earn more zXP, do I get a higher airdrop allocation?**
A: That is exactly how it works. Keep in mind, though, that your exact allocation depends on the zXP earned by others as well. Only 10% of the Total Token Supply (10 million tokens) is available to distribute, so your token allocation depends on your participation and everyone else‚Äôs.
