## SAGE C4 PTR

The SAGE C4 PTR has been running for a little over 1 week, and the team has already pushed many updates and fixes.

This week, we learned a bit more about how **PTR feedback** is being handled behind the scenes. During [last week’s Atlas Brew (#207)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DNiHqliqKqU), John (Product Manager) said that bug reports from the public tracker are being pulled daily into the team’s end-to-end testing system, and that the PTR already has roughly 75% end-to-end user-journey coverage in code. That does not fix the rough edges players are seeing right now, but it does tell us the feedback loop is more structured than it may have looked from the outside.

![SAGE C4 - Recently updated Welcome screen (with a new, disabled option: Solo Training)](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/1e58865ceb0a95c24de009974a3dfbc9896bd6c3893ed6fe2015f978e511b9eb.png)

SAGE C4 – Recently updated Welcome screen (with a new, disabled option: **Solo Training**)

The Star Atlas Discord, meanwhile, saw the arrival of a bot that allows players to get information on certain often-asked-about topics, such as claim stakes. This is meant to help them get their **C4** empire started.

When asked about the functionality of **Cultivation Claims** when SAGE C4 goes live, Michael (CEO) responded that it should at least support resource production at launch, while the full consumable loop remains uncertain. In other words, it’s not yet clear if the stims and end-products of that loop will already have a purpose in the game on day 1.
