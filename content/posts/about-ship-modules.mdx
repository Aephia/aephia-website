Responding to a question about the [Calico Compakt Hero](https://play.staratlas.com/market/6ybME9qMbXgLs3PLWvEv8uyL2LWnUZUz7CYGE4m8kEFm) ship modules, Star Atlas CPO, confirmed that the Hero will only have 2 module slots. Also, added that multi-role ships, will support most of the modules of correct size, “_but could evolve over time with testing. Multi-role_ \[ships\] will _support the most modules, but probably not all of them._“
