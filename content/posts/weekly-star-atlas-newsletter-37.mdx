## Official news

Star Atlas released an article - [How to Prepare for the Upcoming Galactic Marketplace Upgrade](https://medium.com/star-atlas/how-to-prepare-for-the-upcoming-galactic-marketplace-upgrade-6eb03d060bb8) to guide players in anticipation of the Galactic Marketplace upgrade. Players are advised to delist all buy and sell orders. Additionally, once the new Galactic Marketplace goes live, ATMTA will relist assets sold by them with updated ATLAS prices, re-pegging them to the current ATLAS value in USDC. This means that when the new Marketplace comes up, almost all ships offered by the team, from GAO Phase 2, will cost more in ATLAS than they do now, due to the declining value of ATLAS vs USDC since the assets originally were put on the market.

## SCREAM

Chip (Director of Game Systems Design) clarified that ships within fleets will share their aggregate R4 capacity with one another, meaning they will all consume from the same pooled resources. That means when you add any R4 to a ship in the fleet, all ships will benefit from that.

## Progress

The Game economics team has defined the SCREAM macro-economy and sub-economies to determine all their dependencies. Also, the Web team has been building out the movement and cargo functionality, plus they spent time implementing various UI elements.

In an interesting turn of events, Game Design shifted back to the Prototyping phase, whereas they only recently moved to "Building". We'll probably have to wait until next week to see if this was a fluke, or if they went back to the experimentation phase.

## Finance
