You want to build ships in Star Atlas? With the latest [roadmap update](https://staratlas.notion.site/Faction-Aligned-Companies-Public-003dffe8c5c64fe88cce59793dd6ceb5) from the dev team, we learned a lot about the different manufacturers, how to acquire blueprints to craft ships, and much more! To help you navigate all this information, Aephia has published two infographics:

## Star Atlas Ship Chart

[![Aephia StarAtlas ShipChart](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/5e97bbb20b5ad8868c969178970df274865ee2d3a25de809bdd9546543a4dbc1.jpg)](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/5e97bbb20b5ad8868c969178970df274865ee2d3a25de809bdd9546543a4dbc1.jpg "Aephia StarAtlas ShipChart") 

You want to build the ships of a specific manufacturer? This chart shows you everything you need!

## Star Atlas Manufacturer Map

[![Aephia StarAtlas Manufacturer Map](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/5dd2ded71ecbe9d226d5a0a8ddc47c1cbb78f7d603371bab31358a4ce5136095.jpg)](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/5dd2ded71ecbe9d226d5a0a8ddc47c1cbb78f7d603371bab31358a4ce5136095.jpg "Aephia StarAtlas Manufacturer Map") 

This map shows you the location of each manufacturer. As you can see, some of those manufacturers are aligned with more than one faction. The larger part of a triangle shows the majority of a faction for that manufacturer. The smaller part of the triangle shows the minority.
