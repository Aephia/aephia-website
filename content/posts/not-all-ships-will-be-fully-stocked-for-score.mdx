Star Atlas players who purchased their ships before the launch of the SCORE mini-game will enjoy a small benefit:

“Ships that have been sold prior to SCORE rollout (likely to be based on a snapshot date), will be fully stocked with resources in preparation for enlistment. Subsequent ship sales will require resource purchases prior to enlisting”, Star Atlas CEO Michael Wagner announced today.
