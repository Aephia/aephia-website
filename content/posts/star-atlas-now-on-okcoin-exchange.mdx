Star Atlas tokens are now available on [Okcoin Exchange](https://blog.okcoin.com/2021/09/29/star-atlas-is-now-on-okcoin/).

All Okcoin customers excluding residents of the EU, UK, Singapore and Brunei, can now deposit, withdraw and trade ATLAS against USD. Deposits have been live since September 28 and trading from September 29 8pm PT.
