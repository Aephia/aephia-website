## Galactic Marketplace

The big news for the marketplace is that the Blockchain team reported their work is done! Meanwhile the front-end team is still hard at work, but they reported that they will finalize the features planned for release this week! To that end, restricted access has been created that will be shared with the internal and moderator team for training. For the remainder of this week they are currently focusing on the QA process, final design reviews, and potential bug fixes. 

**The team is getting ready to ship!**

## POLIS DAO

The highlight this week is that the Econ0mic team moved into the validation phase, with them finalizing the emissions schedule. On the development front, the front-end team converted the locker function to high-fidelity designs and this week they are finishing the landing page. The blockchain department meanwhile added functions to support the emissions schedule.

## Showroom

Little changed with regards to progress for the Showroom, with Concept Art, UX/UI and Game Design all still officially being listed as *complete*. The Blockchain and Core Gameplay teams did both provide an update, sharing they finalized the wallet and authorization integration. This week the focus will primarily be on performance fine-tuning.

## SCREAM

Last week Chip (Director of Game Systems Design) clarified, in response to a question, that you can command your fleet to defend a starbase while you are offline/sleeping. If that starbase then gets destroyed (and with it, your ships), your fleet will respawn at a different starbase, minus any potential cargo they had on board. Before taking your fleet out again, you will have to repair those ships using Toolkits.

Additionally, when a starbase is destroyed, a small part of the materials used to build/upgrade it will be lootable.

## Progress

For Game Design, the team worked on defining the map, planets, and resource emissions. They also worked with their product manager to define the scope for the pre-alpha release. The only other real update that was provided last week, was that the Web team has been working on building the map and coding various UI elements.
