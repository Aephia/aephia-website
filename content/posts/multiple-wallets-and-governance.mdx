With regards to multiple wallets and [DAC](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/what-is-a-dao/) management, Star Atlas CEO, Michael Wagner, confirmed that multiple wallets will be able to participate in [DAC](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/what-is-a-dao/) management. He stated that “_I had some concerns about the lack of support for multi-sig currently present on Solana, but the response is: ‘for now, our design is \[to\] go around this issue by using a self-defined sequence of approval, which makes it effectively a multi-sig’_ “
