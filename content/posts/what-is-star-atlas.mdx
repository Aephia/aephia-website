![star atlas mini game score start](https://assets.aephia.com/wp-assets/6903acdc42ede7cae216cba7356809dd2edf6c74f3a967aa42a5f3eb443e200e.jpg "star atlas mini game score start")

Now that you know a little bit about the game, Let‚Äôs talk about play to earn. There are four unique asset pillars to the economy of Star Atlas. All of them are represented both on-chain and in-game in some form:

* Land ownership NFTs
* Monetary assets (ATLAS and POLIS, implemented as tokens)
* In-game items (ships, equipment, crew, ship components, buildings, and blueprints, food)
* In-game resources (fungible assets that are produced by miners and consumed by production)

Star Atlas provides a unique opportunity for players to extract in-game virtual earnings into real-world income. Digital assets owned always belong to the holder, and crypto assets earned can be converted into fiat currencies friction-free. Star Atlas has two fungible tokens that fuel all operations: [POLIS](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/star-atlas-polis/) and [ATLAS](https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/star-atlas/). Players can earn these tokens as in-game rewards.
