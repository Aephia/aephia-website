The year 2023, or rather 2623, marked a major milestone in the history of Star Atlas! That year, the Galia Expanse celebrated [100 years of peace](https://medium.com/star-atlas/a-reflection-on-the-100th-anniversary-of-the-signature-of-the-treaty-of-peace-d237dd6af592) between the three major factions, the MUD, the ONI, and the Ustur.

You can read the official address by _Vaor Scarka_ (Ruling Secretary of the Council of Peace) to the citizens of those factions [here](https://medium.com/star-atlas/a-reflection-on-the-100th-anniversary-of-the-signature-of-the-treaty-of-peace-d237dd6af592).

To celebrate this anniversary, we organized a [short story writing competition](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/a-merry-christmas-star-atlas-writing-competition/) in December, themed the fruits of this century of peace, nicely in line with the spirit of Christmas.

The objective was to write a story that would celebrate this peace. It could be an anecdote of something that could only have happened due to the peace treaty.
