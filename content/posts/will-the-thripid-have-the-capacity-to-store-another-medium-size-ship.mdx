Bunthius, aka Danny Floyd, CPO of Star Atlas, confirmed that the Thripid (the only Large and legendary fighter ship sold through the Galactic Asset Offering) does not come with a hangar bay in default config like the Guardian. There will be a module system for a large room within ships, and a hangar bay, is one of them. However, the Thripid does not not have hangars as one of the compatible modules.
