## POLIS Locker

Star Atlas DAO rewards for locking your tokens will go live on August 2nd! To that end, the very first snapshot for the POLIS Locker will be taken on August 1st, at 23:59:59 GMT.

This moment marks another major milestone for the team. It's the first time people will earn POLIS through staking on Star Atlas' own platform. But more importantly, the POLIS Locker marks the first crucial step to allowing the community to determine and influence the future of Star Atlas through the POLIS DAO. You can learn more by visiting the official [Star Atlas DAO page](https://govern.staratlas.com/).
## The Team's Stake

As of the time of writing, the team has staked 8.5 million POLIS on their behalf, with potentially more coming over time as the share of external (non-team) Voting Power (VP) will grow.

Michael (CEO) shared that the team carefully planned their staking strategy to make sure they stay in control of roughly 60-70% of the total Voting Power present in the DAO. That said, the plan is still to stake their full 108 million POLIS allocation over time.

This strategy is beneficial to the community as it means the initial POLIS rewards have a chance to be significantly higher than anticipated, due to a smaller total amount of POLIS locked (and subsequently a lower total VP present in the DAO.

Below is an excerpt from the team's official statement:
> We will be dynamically managing the ATMTA locked tokens to maintain a PVP target weight in the Star Atlas DAO at a ~65% total weight, or enough to provide a buffer against new daily locks, and assuming the actual day-to-day percentage will vary.
> Although the Economics Paper makes clear the team's balance of 108mm POLIS is available for locking, we will not lock the full balance at the outset in order to ensure maximum community benefit and that ATMTA does not collect an overwhelming share of emissions.
> Over time, we intend to lock the entire Team balance as additional POLIS enter the locker, and we will be locking tokens across the 5-year as well as the 4- and 3-year locking period.

## Progress

The team has been busy preparing for the upcoming DAO releases, such as the ATLAS Locker and the proposal/voting mechanisms. The first proposal (PIP - POLIS Improvement Proposal) is being drafted, while the developers are finalizing the ATLAS Locker's design and implementation.

The team is also looking to expand the statistics and information on the reward emissions regarding the POLIS Locker.
## ATLAS Locker

With regards to the ATLAS Locker, Michael has provided a bit more information on what the team is planning:
- Once your ATLAS is staked, it will remain locked for 21 days.
- The more ATLAS you stake, the higher the discount on the upcoming marketplace transaction fees. There will be 5 tiers of discounts on these fees.
- POLIS rewards will be distributed based on a user's total share of ATLAS locked *x* the POLIS emissions curve
- The emission curve matches the one defined for the POLIS Locker, but with 10.8 million POLIS being allocated over the 8-year curve, instead (compared to 127.8 million for the POLIS Locker).

## SCREAM

When asked about faction-specific resources, Michael responded that the team is working on locking some resources to specific factions. However, this might not yet be present in SCREAM v0. The way the team is thinking to accomplish this is by adding tags to the metadata, storing the origin of those resources, and then apply program restrictions on transfer.

## Web3 CEO-CEO Debate

One event that caught many people's attention the past week was the "debate" between Illuvium CEO, Kieran Warwick and Star Atlas CEO, Michael Wagner, moderated by Cagyjan.

The seed for this debate was planted by Kieran on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/kieranwarwick/status/1550252104823869442?s=21&t=w1Rze5v063Wp8LgYZcERNQ) when he attacked Star Atlas by stating they "are going to burn the fuck out of retail". What follows is one hour of Michael, (mostly) patiently explaining Star Atlas to Kieran and the audience, based on questions/accusations from Kieran.
