## Background

On May 10th, the team kicked things off stealthily by sharing the [background story](https://medium.com/star-atlas/prelude-a-galaxy-in-movement-the-first-day-e3c716b1ecde) for the event. In this story, the two core ingredients for the campaign were introduced:

1. The Saand Punaab character, how you (the adventurer in the story) two met, and your objective (return his memory)
2. The Council of Peace (CoP) changing its stance from passive to active/aggressive regarding the ECOS and Jorvik. Where previously, they allowed these minor (pirate) factions to exist outside of the Safe/Secure Zone without any interference from the CoP, after a bunch of brutal incursions, this has now come to an end. The Council of Peace is going to war!

The story combines the two threads, by suggesting that you can contribute to both objectives in one go. By helping the Council wage war on the pirate factions, and bringing Saand while doing so, the events you two will experience may jog his memory and allow him to recover some of his memories.

On May 11th, the team followed up by [launching](https://medium.com/star-atlas/never-alone-a-shared-social-adventure-across-the-star-atlas-galaxy-89cd90627234) the actual campaign, which ties directly into this lore.

## Mission Dashboard

The Campaign is run through an online engagement tool called [Zealy](https://zealy.io/c/staratlas-never-alone/questboard) (formerly known as [Crew3](https://zealy.notion.site/Crew3-is-now-Zealy-d1b2193e3a954b85a0845d4a41472b40)). The team defined a whole bunch of quests in Zealy, and when you go there, you will simply see a mission dashboard, containing those quests, waiting for you to engage with them. Most of these you can easily complete through links in the quests or by providing an answer to a question (both lore & personal info like Twitter profile & wallet). However, there are also quests that require you to use the supplied wallet to engage with other applications the team will launch this year. For this first Mission, the focus lies on Escape Velocity!

## Missions

Never Alone consist of 4 chapters, or missions. Each mission will have its own quests and lore and is crafted around a product the Star Atlas will launch this year. This does not mean that all the quests for that mission will be solely focused on that product, there will be plenty of more general quests introduced with each mission.

### Quests

Each mission consists of a number of (chains of ) quests. In order to complete any of them, you will first have to have completed prerequisite quests. Mission 1 starts with a single quest in the Intro category that has to be completed before other quests and other categories become available.

Each quest comes with a small snippet of lore to describe what is happening. Completing a quest requires you to answer a multiple-choice question, an open question (which will trigger a review), a social-media action (Twitter), or a Discord action (claiming role). The first mission, another mechanic is moving ships around in Escape Velocity.

Straight-up multiple-choice quiz questions and social media engagement (like/RT) will be verified on the spot. However, when it comes to open-ended questions (like your Solana wallet â€“ the very first quest), or posting specific content on Twitter/YouTube, your answer will need to be reviewed by a member of the team. This may take a while, so best to plan for that.

The quests are divided into multiple categories, each one containing one or several quest chains. For the first mission, these are:

* Intro
* Diplomacy
* Exploration
* Research
* Mission 1 â€“ Escape Velocity

## DAC Competition

There is a whole second layer to this Campaign, and that focuses exclusively on Decentralized Autonomous Corporations, or DACs (more widely known as _guilds_). Every DAC member that redeems 3 memories of the same tier at the end of the year, will also contribute points to their DAC. The more points a DAC gets, the higher their score and the better their chance to win some reward(s) for their guild.

Each tier of memories redeemed is worth a different amount of points. If you redeem multiple, then you will get multiple asset bundles (see previous section), and your DAC will accumulate more points.
