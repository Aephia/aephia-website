## The Road to z.ink ‚Äì O.RIGIN

The A.LPHA campaign concluded this past week, so it‚Äôs no longer possible to collect zXP. The team extended the grace period for daily check-ins, so over the past few days, it was no longer necessary to return daily to maintain the check-in streak. Daily check-ins are still possible, however, so it is (and has been) possible to increase the streak length (which is helpful if you accidentally broke it at some point).

But the big news: The z.ink O.RIGIN campaign kicks off **tomorrow**! On Tuesday, December 16th, the campaign will officially launch, and everyone will be able to grind for that initial 10% (of the total supply) z.ink airdrop!

Unlike the A.LPHA campaign, there will no longer be a ceiling on the maximum attainable level.
In the A.LPHA campaign, 14 community members reached or exceeded level 20\. Their zXP and level will be capped exactly at the level 20 mark before the O.RIGIN campaign kicks off.

### Claim Stakes

The team made a last-minute addition to the roster of zXP-earning activities. Staking Claim Stakes (in the Faction Claims program) will now earn you zXP as well (when O.RIGIN kicks off, that is)!
As with the Locker programs, the actual amount of zXP rewarded to each participant will be calculated once per day, at approximately midnight UTC.
