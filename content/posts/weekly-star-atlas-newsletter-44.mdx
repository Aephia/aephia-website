We had another quiet week in Star Atlas development, with not much new info being shared by the team. The lore team did, as always, share their [Roadmap Scan (#18)](https://medium.com/star-atlas/the-truth-about-gerio-part-iii-star-atlas-roadmap-scan-18-6b25cbcab4ab) however, shedding more light on the mysterious origins of the creature Atoph.

## Star Atlas DAO

The team spent the pst week fixing  some bugs in the ATLAS Locker that were discovered earlier. The plan is to wrap that up this week.

Perhaps more exciting is that development is on-going on a dark mode for the DAO portal and the creation and publication of developer tools to interact with the POLIS-, and (upcoming) ATLAS Lockers.

Lastly the product design team is working on the design for Proposals.
