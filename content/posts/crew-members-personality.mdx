In answering to a question about crew personality effects, Star Atlas CPO, Danny Floyd, confirmed that “_all crew will benefit from each other. Although individuals have an [OCEAN](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/BigFivepersonalitytraits) rating and so there’s personality management. Some crew can clash with each other which debuffs._” Same are planned for implementation when the ship-configurable missions of the browser-based mini-game come online.
