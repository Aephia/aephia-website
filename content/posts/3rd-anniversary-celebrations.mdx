## Starbased Guides

Back when SAGE Labs was launched, we conceived an ambitious plan to compose a multi-part guide to help new players on their way. Unfortunately, time constraints resulted in only [Part 1](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/sage-labs-the-missing-manual-part-1/) having ever been published.

When Starbased was released, we created an [Upgrade/Migration guide](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/starbased-the-missing-manual/) (of sorts, which highlighted the various updates and improvements that Starbased added to the mix.

However, this did not really help new players. Part 1 of the SAGE Labs manual was really not helpful as the UI drastically changed with Starbased.

This past week, we finally remedied that! We created a brand new [Part 1](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/starbased-the-missing-manual-part-1/), which essentially covers the same topics as the previous manual. We also created a new, in-depth intro to the mini-game with [Part 0](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/starbased-the-missing-manual-part-0/). On top of that, we finally managed to expand the series with [Part 2](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/starbased-the-missing-manual-part-2/), which covers _mining_, _scanning_, and _crafting_.

What remains now is the last part, Part 3\. We plan to publish that one in a matter of weeks, rounding out the 4-part tutorial and offering new players a single guide that goes over 95% of the Starbased gameplay. Fortunately for us, itâ€™s a relatively low lift as our [Upgrade Manual](https://aephia.com/star-atlas/starbased-the-missing-manual/) already contains 95% of the content.

## Cataclysm

Since our original announcement in late 2022, many community members have been asking us about our upcoming Community Game, **Cataclysm: Battle For Iris** (previously: Project Cataclysm).
