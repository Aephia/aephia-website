## Galactic Marketplace v2

The new Marketplace was rolled out shortly after the 426Live event concluded, delivering an experience that was rebuilt from the ground up visually, and more importantly on the blockchain as well.

The most important features are:
- No more claiming/settling of orders - Your purchases appear in your wallet instantly
- No more Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) - Which should prevent people from trying to buy a "buy"-order
- Looks more like an Auction House, known to gamers from various MMOs

## StarPath

The team released their referral program, shortly after the marketplace went live. This program intends to incentivize the community by rewarding them for bringing new players into the ecosystem.

Everyone can register to get a referral link, which -when used by someone who connects their wallet to the website- will automatically connect that person with the account that created the link.

If people make purchases from Star Atlas directly, then the person who referred them will earn Prosperity Marks. Once someone has earned enough of these, they can choose to exchange them for Star Atlas ships.

## DAO Portal & POLIS Locker

At 426Live, Michael described what DAO governance will bring to Star Atlas and its community. He went through what proposals and voting would look like in the near future (when that will be released) and zoomed in on the POLIS Locker that launched simultaneously with the portal as its first feature.
## POLIS Locker

The POLIS Locker is now open for everyone to stake their POLIS. It can be locked anywhere between 2 weeks to 5 years (there are 10 locking duration options to select from).

On August 1st a snapshot will be taken, after which the daily rewards (in POLIS) will start dropping from August 2nd onwards.

## Hardware partnership with iBUYPOWER

Early on during the 426Live event, the team announced a new partnership, concerning PC hardware. iBUYPOWER - known for sponsoring the exclusive Star Atlas branded PC during COPA - will start selling limited edition PCs, headsets and desk pads.

First, though, they want to know if there is enough interest. To that end, they launched an [online form](https://ibuypower.com/community/partners/star-atlas) where people can select the product bundle they would like to acquire. The next step will be spinning up production, starting in North America, with other regions following two weeks later (provided there was enough interest).
