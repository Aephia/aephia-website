## IMPACT ‚Äì Star Atlas Summit

Preparations for the first-ever Star Atlas conference, the IMPACT Summit, are well underway! This full-day conference will take place on September 21st, starting at 10 AM and wrapping up at 6 PM. The team is planning various team presentations, sneak peeks, and fireside chats with team members throughout the day! On top of that, the Hackathon winners will be announced, and there is a Surge tournament going on in which attendees can participate, with the finals being live-streamed on stage.

The whole event will also be live-streamed through YouTube, so you can stay fully up to speed, even from the confines of your home (or office)! This also means you can catch a recording later if you cannot tune in live.

If you are attending, please [register using this Lu.ma link](https://lu.ma/3728vl8l). Over 500 others have already done so, but the expectation is that many more Breakpoint attendees will also‚Äìat least briefly‚Äìexplore what is going on the floor beneath them. Especially with access being free for all!

The team has also announced that all attendees will get a free Ogrika Rucha IMPACT Skin to add to their collection!

## Naabathon

The [Naabathon](https://naabathon.staratlas.com/), Star Atlas‚Äô first hackathon, is coming to an end. The deadline to [submit your project](https://naabathon.staratlas.com/) is this Friday, September 7th. The winners will be announced during the IMPACT Summit on September 21st.

There are $50k worth of prizes, including various ships (valued at their VWAP price). The grand winner will take home an Ogrika Sunpaa (large) vessel!
